# Code of Conduct

Agent-Native OS is a place for careful technical discussion about agent-native systems, governance, and long-running AI work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Critique ideas, designs, and tradeoffs rather than people.
- Assume good faith, but ask for clarity when claims are unclear.
- Keep examples neutral and avoid exposing private workflows, credentials, or confidential data.
- Welcome contributors with different backgrounds, models, tooling preferences, and implementation targets.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Misrepresenting speculative ideas as verified project guarantees.
- Submitting hidden vendor lock-in, undisclosed telemetry, or unsafe mutation behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when needed to keep the project productive and safe.

If you see a problem, contact the maintainers privately when possible. For urgent security issues, follow `SECURITY.md`.
